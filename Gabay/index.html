Product

Features of GABAY Smartcane

GABAY transforms the traditional white cane into an intelligent, sensor-driven navigation assistant — paired with a web interface for caregivers and users alike.

Intelligent Obstacle Detection

Uses advanced sensors to detect obstacles in real time.

Adaptive Haptic Feedback

Communicates through vibration patterns, allowing users to “feel” their surroundings

GPS Navigation Support

Outdoor navigation is powered by GPS, allowing route planning for familiar and unfamiliar environments.

Standalone Operation

Core obstacle detection and navigation work fully offline — no internet required. The cane operates standalone even without the web interface connected.

Companion Web Interface

Caregivers and users can input destinations, view routes, and optionally monitor location — all through a clean, accessible web-based dashboard via BLE or Wi-Fi.

About Us

Built by students, for the community

We are a team driven by one simple belief: technology should include everyone. GABAY was created to bridge the gap between innovation and accessibility, giving visually impaired individuals the freedom to navigate the world with confidence and dignity.

By combining smart technology with purposeful design, we aim to turn everyday challenges into opportunities for independence. GABAY is not just a product, it’s a step toward a more inclusive future, where no one is left behind.

Mission

To develop affordable, IoT-powered assistive technology that promotes safe, independent mobility for visually impaired individuals.

Vision

A world where every visually impaired person can navigate their surroundings safely and confidently with the help of smart technology.

Why GABAY stands apart

Most smart navigation aids are expensive and designed for foreign environments. GABAY is built locally, priced affordably, and tested in real Philippine conditions.

01

Locally Adapted

Developed and tested in Philippine environments — roads, weather, and terrain that other foreign products weren't designed for.

02

Affordable & Accessible

A low-cost alternative to expensive international assistive tech, designed to be accessible to families across all income levels.

03

Holistic Safety System

From obstacle detection to caregiver monitoring — GABAY covers the full spectrum of mobility safety in one integrated device.

04

Independence-First

Designed to empower users to move on their own — not just to alert others. The cane is a personal navigation assistant, not just a tracker.

05

SDG-Aligned

06

Target Users

Who GABAY serves

Designed with two key groups in mind — those who use the cane and those who care for them.

Visually Impaired Individuals

People who navigate daily environments with limited or no sight — GABAY's core users.

  • Adults with partial or total vision loss
  • Urban and semi-urban users across the Philippines
  • Independent or semi-independent travelers
  • White cane users seeking a smarter alternative
  • Those needing real-time, hands-free guidance

Caregivers & Family Members

Secondary users of the companion web interface who support and monitor loved ones.

  • Parents and family members
  • Professional caregivers and social workers
  • School and institution support staff
  • Healthcare workers monitoring mobility
  • Organizations aligned with SDG 3 & SDG 10
